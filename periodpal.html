Best option: full JSON export
Use period_app_import_data.json. It imports confirmed cycles, the pregnancy exclusion, daily events, notes, measurements, medication logs, and sex history.

start date, end date, flow, symptoms, notes
Example: 2026-01-03,2026-01-07,medium,cramps|fatigue,rough cramps day 1
End date, symptoms, and notes can be blank.
